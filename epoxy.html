‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is epoxy grouting better than cement grouting?
A: Yes. Epoxy offers stronger bonding, chemical resistance, and lasts much longer than traditional cement grouts.

Q: Where is epoxy grouting commonly used?
A: It is used in basements, water tanks, heavy-duty floors, machine foundations, and RCC structural repairs.

Q: Can it stop water leakage through walls?
A: Yes, especially when the leakage is due to structural cracks or chemical attack.
