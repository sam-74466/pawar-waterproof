Sunken Slab Waterproofing

Leak-proof protection for bathrooms and toilets with sunken slabs.

Why Sunken Slab Waterproofing Is Important

Sunken slabs store plumbing lines and water accumulation often leads to seepage into lower floors. Proper waterproofing avoids slab damage and ceiling leakage.
