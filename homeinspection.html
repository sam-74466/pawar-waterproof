Complete Home Inspection Checklist

Our professional home inspection service covers structural safety, waterproofing, plumbing, electrical systems and interior finishing. This inspection helps identify hidden defects before buying a flat or property.

1. Structural Inspection

Structural inspection ensures the building strength and detects cracks or structural damage.

  • Major wall cracks
  • Column cracks
  • Beam cracks
  • Slab damage
  • Floor slope level
  • Ceiling cracks
  • Balcony slab condition
  • Structural settlement signs
  • Plaster hollow areas
  • Load bearing wall condition

2. Waterproofing & Leakage Inspection

Waterproofing inspection identifies leakage problems, damp walls and seepage issues that may damage the structure and interiors.

  • Terrace waterproofing condition
  • Bathroom leakage signs
  • Kitchen sink leakage
  • Damp wall detection
  • Window water seepage
  • Balcony waterproofing
  • Pipe leakage
  • Ceiling damp patches
  • Plumbing joints leakage
  • External wall seepage

3. Plumbing System Inspection

Plumbing inspection ensures proper water flow, drainage and pipe condition.

  • Water pressure check
  • Pipe leakage check
  • Drainage slope
  • Bathroom drain flow
  • Kitchen drainage
  • Toilet flush system
  • Pipe corrosion signs
  • Water tank connection
  • Tap fittings condition
  • Sewer smell detection

4. Electrical System Inspection

Electrical inspection checks wiring safety, load capacity and electrical equipment condition.

  • Main electrical panel
  • Circuit breaker condition
  • Earthing check
  • Switchboard condition
  • Socket safety
  • Wiring exposure
  • Voltage testing
  • Lighting fixtures condition
  • AC power point wiring
  • Electrical load safety

5. Interior Finishing Inspection

Interior inspection identifies finishing defects, tile issues and installation quality.

  • Tile hollow sound
  • Tile alignment
  • Door alignment
  • Window sealing
  • Paint quality
  • Wall finishing
  • Skirting finishing
  • False ceiling condition
  • Cabinet installation quality
  • Floor finishing defects

6. Safety & Utility Inspection

Safety inspection ensures the property is safe for living and meets essential utility requirements.

  • Balcony railing safety
  • Fire safety provision
  • Ventilation check
  • Natural lighting
  • Gas pipeline safety
  • Lift access safety
  • Staircase condition
  • Water supply availability
  • Parking area condition
  • Overall property risk rating
