  1. Tank Surface Cleaning & Inspection: We clean the tank thoroughly and inspect for cracks, honeycombs, or weak spots.
  2. Crack Filling & Surface Repair: All cracks and joints are sealed using non-toxic sealants or cementitious mortars.
  3. Surface Priming: A compatible primer is applied to enhance the bonding of the waterproof layer.
  4. Waterproof Coating Application: Two or more coats of food-grade, non-toxic waterproofing material are applied on all surfaces.
  5. Curing & Testing: After proper curing, we fill the tank partially to check for any leakage or coating failure.
