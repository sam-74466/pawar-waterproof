Q: What can thermal scanning detect?
A: It detects hidden moisture, water leaks, electrical overheating, and insulation gaps without damaging surfaces.

Q: Is thermal scanning safe?
A: Yes, it uses infrared radiation, which is completely safe and non-destructive.

Q: How long does a typical scan take?
A: It usually takes 30 to 60 minutes depending on the area and complexity of inspection.

Q: Do I need to switch off my power or AC systems?
A: No. In fact, active systems help identify heat signatures more accurately.

Q: Is thermal scanning useful before waterproofing?
A: Absolutely. It helps identify moisture sources and leakage points precisely before waterproofing work begins.
