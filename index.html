Epoxy Grouting Waterproofing

Seal cracks, voids & joints with strong, chemical-resistant epoxy grouting by Pawar Waterproofing. Ideal for industrial floors, basements, and structural repairs with Pawar Waterproofing’s expert.

+

✅ Why Epoxy Grouting Is Important

  • Provides high-strength bonding in structural cracks
  • Resistant to chemicals, oils, water & heat
  • Prevents further concrete deterioration
  • Seals hairline and deep cracks permanently
  • Increases lifespan of concrete floors, tanks, & beams

🔧 Our Epoxy Grouting Process

  1. Crack & Void Assessment: We examine the structure to find surface and deep cracks using inspection tools.
  2. Cleaning & Surface Prep: Cracks are cleaned with brushes or air blowers to remove dust, oil, or debris.
  3. Port Installation or Gravity Pour: For fine cracks, we use injection ports. For surface voids, epoxy is gravity poured.
  4. Epoxy Injection/Grouting: Two-component epoxy is injected under pressure or poured into cracks.
  5. Finishing & Curing: Surface is cleaned and allowed to cure fully for 24–48 hours.

🧰 Materials We Use in Epoxy Grouting

Fosroc Lokfix / Nitomortar / Nitofill EP

Sika Injection EP / Sikadur-52

Dr. Fixit Epoxy Grout

STP & MC-Bauchemie Epoxy Range

(Material is selected based on crack width, structure load, and chemical exposure)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is epoxy grouting better than cement grouting?
A: Yes. Epoxy offers stronger bonding, chemical resistance, and lasts much longer than traditional cement grouts.

Q: Where is epoxy grouting commonly used?
A: It is used in basements, water tanks, heavy-duty floors, machine foundations, and RCC structural repairs.

Q: Can it stop water leakage through walls?
A: Yes, especially when the leakage is due to structural cracks or chemical attack.

podium waterproofing

Protect your residential or commercial podium slab from water seepage, tile damage, and structural cracks with advanced podium waterproofing solutions by Pawar Waterproofing.

+

✅ Why Podium Waterproofing Is Important

  • Prevents water leakage to basement parking or lower floors
  • Protects landscape tiles, planters, and concrete slab from water damage
  • Ensures durability of waterproofing under heavy foot or vehicle traffic
  • Avoids algae formation, cracks, and long-term concrete deterioration
  • Reduces costly repairs and structural risk in large complexes

🔧 Our Podium Waterproofing Process

  1. Inspection & Surface Cleaning: The entire slab is checked for cracks, ponding, and joints. Surface is cleaned of dust, mortar, and old coating.
  2. Crack Filling & Joint Sealing: Expansion joints, tile gaps, and minor cracks are sealed with PU sealant or epoxy grout.
  3. Primer & Reinforced Waterproofing Layer:Waterproof primer is applied, followed by multiple layers of elastomeric (flexible) waterproof membrane, often with geotextile fabric.
  4. Final Leak Testing & Handing Over: Water ponding test is conducted to ensure complete leak proofing before project completion.

    5. 🧰 Materials We Use in Podium Waterproofing

    Fosroc Nitoproof 600 / Brush bond RFX

    Sika WP Sheet Membranes / Raintite System

    Asian Paints SmartCare Damp Proof / PU systems

    (Materials are chosen based on usage load, planter areas, and slope direction)

    ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What is a podium slab?
    A:It is the elevated slab area over basement parking or connecting towers, often used for garden, tiles, or driveways.

    Q:Why does water leak through tiles on podium?
    A: Improper slope, open tile joints, and lack of membrane under tiles cause seepage.

    Q: Can I waterproof my podium without removing tiles?
    A: Only if the leakage is mild. But for long-term results, tile removal and full membrane application is best.

    Q: Is podium waterproofing expensive?

crack filling .

Seal visible and hidden cracks to prevent water seepage, dampness, and long-term structural damage with Pawar Waterproofing’s expert crack filling service.

+

✅ Why Crack Filling Is Important

  • Stops water leakage through walls, ceilings, and slabs
  • Prevents growth of algae, Mold, and damp patches
  • Protects internal structure from rusting and corrosion
  • Restores aesthetic finish of your surfaces
  • Essential for the longevity of waterproofing systems

🔧 Our Crack Filling Process

  1. Crack Inspection & Mapping: We inspect and mark all cracks—horizontal, vertical, or structural—for appropriate treatment.
  2. Crack Widening & Cleaning: Cracks are opened slightly (V-grooving) and cleaned to ensure deep penetration of filler.
  3. Application of Crack Filler: High-strength acrylic/polymer-based fillers are injected or filled using putty knives.
  4. Sanding & Surface Finishing: After curing, the filled cracks are sanded and levelled for a smooth finish.
  5. Protective Coating (Optional): An elastomeric or waterproof coating is applied over the area for added protection.

    6. 🧰 Materials We Use in Crack Filling

    Dr. Fixit Crack-X Shrinkfree / Crack-X Paste

    Asian Paints SmartCare Crack Seal

    Fosroc Renderoc FC / Nitofill

    Sika CrackSeal / Acrylic Filler

    (Choice of material depends on crack size, location, and surface type)

    ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: Will cracks reopen after filling?
    A:No, if done with flexible and high-quality material, filled cracks stay sealed for years.

    Q: Can you fill ceiling or slab cracks too?
    A: Yes, we handle vertical, horizontal, and even overhead crack filling.

    Q: What if the crack is too wide or deep?
    A: We use special injection grouts or PU fillers for wider or structural cracks.

    Q: How long does crack filling last?
    A: Typically 5–8 years depending on surface movement and environmental conditions.

    Q: Is crack filling needed before waterproofing?
    A: Yes, crack treatment is a vital step before any waterproofing or paint work.
